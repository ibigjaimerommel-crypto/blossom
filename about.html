About Us

Blossom & Bloom Co. sprouted from the innovative minds of five third-year students driven by a shared passion: to harmonize environmental stewardship with entrepreneurial spirit. Frustrated by the disconnect between profit-driven industries and ecological well-being, these students envisioned a business that could simultaneously benefit the planet and generate sustainable income.
